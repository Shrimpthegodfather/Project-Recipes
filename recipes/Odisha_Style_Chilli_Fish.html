Odisha Style Chilli Fish

Chili fish is a popular dish in Chinese and Indo-Chinese cuisine, typically made by frying fish fillets and then tossing them in a spicy, flavorful sauce. The dish often features a combination of chili, garlic, soy sauce, and sometimes ginger, giving it a bold, savory, and spicy taste.

Ingredients

  • 300 gm Fish
  • handful coriander leaves
  • 150 gm Capsicum
  • 200 gm onion
  • 3 green chili
  • 1 tomato
  • 4-5 Chopped garlic
  • 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1 tsp each Soya sause, tomato sauce, Chili sause, vinegar
  • 150 gm Corn flour
  • as required salt
  • 100 ml Refined oli
  • 1/2 tsp Michi powder, haldi powder

Steps

  1. First marinate the fish with cornflour and food color and ginger garlic paste and salt
  2. Then fried it
  3. Put the kadai then add refined oil
  4. Add ginger garlic paste, chopped green chilli, chopped onion and capsicum
  5. After 5 mins add chopped tomato
  6. Add all sause mention above 1 spoon each
  7. Add haldi 1 pinch, half teaspoon mirchi powder and salt as per taste
  8. After 15 mins add fried fish to it. Then stir.
  9. After 10 mins add coriander leaves
  10. And your delicous chili fish is ready to be served
